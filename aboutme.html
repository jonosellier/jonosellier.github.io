A little bit about me

My name is Jonathan Sellier. I am currently a student at University of South Florida in Tampa, Florida pursuing a degree in Computer Science. I have always been interested in coding and general computing, even using a computer from as young as 2 years old.

I am using this site as both an online portfolio/resume and also as a central hub for my other creative works like written tutorials and summaries of some of the finer topics in computer science.

I consider myself highly motivated and truly love creating side projects and learning new languages, frameworks, and means development strategies. My current love is using JavaScript for cross-platform development through Progressive Web Apps. I truly hope that this means of development takes hold and allows for experiences to be brought to users regardless of their platform.
